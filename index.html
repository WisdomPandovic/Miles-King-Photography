Photography

Taking pictures is savoring life intensely, every hundredth of a second.

Photography is a way of feeling, of touching, of loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever… It remembers little things, long after you have forgotten everything Taking an image, freezing a moment, reveals how rich reality truly is. There is one thing the photograph must contain, the humanity of the moment.

The picture that you took with your camera is the imagination you want to create with reality. If the photographer is interested in the people in front of his lens, and if he is compassionate, it’s already a lot. The instrument is not the camera but the photographer.
